    About Us

    St. Wesina Education Centre is a comprehensive learning community offering Nursery, Primary, Secondary, and University pathways in Sironko, Uganda. We integrate creativity, technology, and character to prepare learners for a changing world.

    • Royal Blue & Gold identity rooted in excellence
    • Hands-on science and creative studios
    • Student-centered mentorship and guidance
    • Community engagement and leadership
